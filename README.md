**Event-Driven Resilient Architecture for Extreme Ocean Environments**

MARISYN is an event-driven autonomous deep-sea exploration system designed to operate reliably under **extreme hydrostatic pressure, sudden underwater disturbances, and long-term material fatigue**. The platform introduces a **mother-shell + intelligent pod architecture** that activates sensing and deployment only when meaningful biological, chemical, or pressure anomalies are detected, enabling energy-efficient and failure-resilient ocean missions.

## âœ¨ Our Solution: "Event-Driven Autonomous Ocean Intelligence"
MARISYN introduces a resilient exploration pipeline:

1. **Dormant-by-Default Operation:** System remains inactive to conserve energy until anomalies are detected.
2. **Mother Shell + Autonomous Pods:** Risk-separated architecture prevents total mission failure.
3. **Passive Bio-Inspired Sensing:** Non-invasive sensing inspired by pressure-tolerant marine life.
4. **Tetherless Navigation:** Autonomous operation without GPS or physical tethering.
5. **Self-Recharge Mechanism:** Pods return to the mother shell for energy replenishment.
